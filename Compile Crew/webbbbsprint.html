About Us

Tayacan Watches is a brand synonymous with luxury, precision, and timeless elegance. Founded in 2023, our mission is to create watches that not only tell time but also tell a story. Each piece is a testament to our commitment to quality and craftsmanship, designed for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

The Craftsmanship

A Legacy of Excellence

Each Tayacan timepiece represents over 150 years of watchmaking tradition. Our master craftsmen spend up to 6 months creating a single watch, ensuring unparalleled quality and precision.

Meticulous Attention to Detail

Every component is hand-finished and polished to perfection. The movement consists of over 300 individual parts, each inspected under a microscope for flawless performance.

Sustainable Luxury

We source our materials ethically, using recycled precious metals and responsibly harvested leather. Each timepiece is designed to last generations, embodying our commitment to sustainable luxury.

Limited Production

We produce only 500 pieces annually, ensuring exclusivity and maintaining our rigorous quality standards. Each watch is individually numbered and comes with a certificate of authenticity.
